Apply claim

Apply claim at teller​

  1. First you need to login to teller.mmis.ounch.com with your username and password.

  1. Click claim to navigate into the page.

  1. Click create claim to create a new claim.

  1. Fill in the details of claim and proceed to the next step.

  1. Fill in the identity details and submit.

Apply claim at admin​

  1. First you need to login to admin.mmis.ounch.com with your username and password.

  1. Click Create New Claim to navigate into the page.

  1. Step 1 - fill up all the required fields then click on Proceed to Next Step.
    • MTA Unique ID*
    • Client Name*
    • ID Number*

  1. Step 2 - process:
    • Click on Add Attachments
    • Fill up the required fields & upload file
    • Click on Add Attachment button

  1. Step 3 - fill up all the required fields then click on Proceed to Next Step OR Save & Exit.

  1. Step 4 - fill up all the required fields then click on Proceed to Next Step OR Save & Exit.
    • Date Request
    • Type of Transfer
    • Amount
    • Date Claim Paid to Client
    • Bank Account
    • Bank Name

  1. Step 5 - fill up all the required fields then click on Proceed to Next Step OR Save & Exit.
    • Date of payment to MTA Account
    • Receivables
    • Amount
    • Remarks

  1. Check the details and save to complete the claim.
