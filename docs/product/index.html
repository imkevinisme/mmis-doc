Product creation

Product creation​

Create product - Step 1​

  1. First you need to login to admin.mmis.ounch.com with your username and password.

  1. Click client to navigate into the page.

  1. Click create product to create a product.

  1. Fill in the details to complete the first step for complete the creation.

Create plans - Step 2​

  1. Click plans to navigate it into the page.

  1. Click plans product to create a plan.

  1. Fill in the details and proceed to the next step.

  1. Click add method to fill in payment method and proceed to the next step.

  1. Click add identifications to fill in nationalities and proceed to the next step.

  1. Click add benefits to fill in benefits and proceed to the next step.

  1. Click add benefits to fill in benefits and proceed to the next step.

  1. Click add underwriting questions to fill in questions and proceed to the next step.

  1. Tick the button to confirm the details, save and active to complete a product creation.
  • "save" is saving the draft for now

Active product - Step 3​

  1. Click plans to navigate it into the page.

  1. Click edit to navigate into the page

  1. Select INACTIVE to ACTIVE
